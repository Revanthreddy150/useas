My Love Letter ❤️

Nuvvu naa life lo vachinappati nunchi anni marchipoyanu. Na days anni special ga anipisthunnayi, endhukante naa thoughts lo ekkuvaga nuvve untav.

Ninnu first time chusinappudu ila feel avuthanu ani anukoledu. Kani mellaga nuvvu naa heart lo chala special place teesukunnaav.

Nee smile chusthe naa badha motham marchipotha. Nee maatlu vinte naa mind ki peace vasthundi.

Nijam cheppali ante, ninnu chala ishtapaduthunna. Ee feeling just attraction kadhu… idi heart nunchi vachina pure love.

Nuvvu happy ga unte naku chala santosham. Nee face lo smile undali ani eppudu korukunta.

Ninu chusina kshanam nunchi alochinchani nimisham ledhu. Prathi roju, prathi nimisham naa mind lo nuvve untav.

Nuvvu daggara unte time chala fast ga velthundi, kani nuvvu dooram unte prathi second chala long ga anipisthundi.

Naa life lo chala mandi vacharu, vellipoyaru. Kani nuvvu matram chala special. Endhukante nuvee naa first love.

Ee mata cheppadaniki konchem bayam ga unna, naa heart lo unna nijam matram idhe.

Life long nee pakkane undali, nee happiness lo share avvali, nee badhalu kuda naavi ani feel avvali anukuntunna.

Naa life lo nuvvu chala important. Cheppadaniki words saripovu kani oka mata cheptha…

I Love You ❤️
Nuvvu naa life lo undadam naa biggest happiness.
